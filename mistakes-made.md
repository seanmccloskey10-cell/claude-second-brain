# Mistakes Log

When Claude makes a mistake, or when you make a decision you want to learn from, log it here. This is a **write-only log** — Claude does NOT read it at session start. Instead, durable lessons get promoted into CLAUDE.md as permanent rules. The full log is reviewed during weekly briefings.

**Rules:**
- Newest entries at the top
- Never delete entries — this is append-only
- Be specific about what went wrong and what to do differently
- If a lesson is durable (will matter in future sessions), also add it as a rule in CLAUDE.md

**Format:**
```
## YYYY-MM-DD — Short title

**What happened:** What went wrong, in plain language.
**What to do differently:** The concrete lesson.
**Tags:** #category
```
