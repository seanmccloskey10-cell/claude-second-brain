## What This Is

Strategies and observations about keeping existing clients engaged and coming back. For a tutoring business, this means understanding why students stay, why they leave, and what makes the difference.

## Key Ideas

- Retention is cheaper than acquisition — it costs 5-10x more to find a new client than to keep an existing one
- The biggest driver of churn isn't price, it's perceived progress — if students don't feel like they're improving, they leave regardless of how much they like you
- Regular check-ins ("how are you finding the sessions?") catch dissatisfaction before it becomes a cancellation
- Students who have a clear goal (exam prep, career change, specific project) stay longer than those exploring
